05 — Engagement · Align
Align1 SPRINT (2 WEEKS) · FIXED SCOPE

A standalone discovery engagement. We get in before there's a brief, ask the questions that prevent expensive pivots later, and hand you a clear point of view on where the work actually needs to go.

  • 2–3 working sessions with your core stakeholders
  • Landscape and competitive scan
  • A point of view on where design, strategy, motion, and production each apply to your problem
  • A recommended path into Advance or Ascend, with a rough sprint roadmap
Start here if: you know something needs to change but haven't scoped what yet.

06 — Engagement · Advance
AdvanceMIN. 2 SPRINTS · ROLLING

One or two disciplines actively producing, the rest on call. Renews at sprint boundaries — no long lock-in.

  • One or two pillars leading (e.g., strategy + design), the other two consulted as needed
  • One pillar-led output per sprint — positioning doc, first-pass expression, campaign concept
  • Weekly async check-in
  • Sprint-close summary and next-sprint recommendation
Start here if: the direction is set and you need senior hands moving it forward.
Advance07 / 11
07 — Engagement · Ascend
AscendMIN. 3 SPRINTS · CONTINUOUS

All four pillars active in parallel, every sprint. The closest thing we offer to a fractional senior team.

  • Design, strategy, motion, and production all working simultaneously
  • Dedicated PM/traffic across the group
  • Weekly live sync, not async
  • Production-ready output each sprint, not just concepts
  • Monthly retro that doubles as a client-facing wrap-up moment
Start here if: you need an embedded team carrying the work end to end.
